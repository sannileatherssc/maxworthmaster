    • Company Profile

    MAXWORTH ELECTRONIC SYSTEMS PVT LTD, established in 1993 is a leading and reputed provider of innovative and end-to-end solutions in the Auto-Identification & Data Capture (AIDC) sector. With a sharp focus on customized & turnkey solutions for today’s ever-changing and competitive business environment, we have provided some of the most innovative solutions to leading organizations in Government and Private Sector.
    Maxworth demonstrates an end-to-end commitment with quality products and services from the pilot to implementation, commissioning, and after-sales. We aim to exceed customer expectations by constantly monitoring, benchmarking, and improving our business, products & services to meet growing challenges.
    Our innovative solutions using the latest technologies like Barcode, RFID, IoT, Mobile computing, wireless data communication & Cloud computing have earned us a steady and satisfied stream of customers in the Government, Public Service & Safety, Aviation, Telecommunication, Warehousing & Manufacturing sectors.
    In our endeavour to provide our customers with the best possible solutions, we are proud to have partnered with the world’s major global manufacturers of hardware, including Honeywell, Zebra, ICTS Europe, Access, CISCO, HP and DT Research for Barcode scanners, Rugged Mobile Computers, Tablets, RFID readers and Label & Card printers.
    With a robust pan-India base with headquarters in New Delhi and now an expanding presence in the ASEAN countries, including Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, and Philippines, we are growing from strength to strength with a 100 plus workforce including a large in-house software development team and are fully geared up to meet your challenging requirements.
    We aspire to be true to our organizational motto, of being able to build a swift, smart and secure business ecosystem for any institution, anywhere in the world.

Leadership Team




  • SANJAY GARG
    Sanjay Garg is the quintessential businessperson. He graduated from IT BHU with a Bachelors in Electronics. He worked in Indian and Multinational company’s garnering a wealth of experience before plunging into the entrepreneurial bandwagon. Philips India and Crompton Greaves honed his skills which he used to found and grow Maxworth into the ‘ Enterprise Mobility “ solutions provider that it has become today. Simple living and high thinking are his motto. Family and healthy lifestyles are very close to his heart. He believes in Karma as enunciated in the Bhagwat Gita - Karm karo – Phal ki Ichha mat karo- Safalta to jhak marke piche aayegi. Follow one’s passion with utmost humility and honesty and the results will follow.

  • AJAY JHINGRAN
    Ajay, the founder director of Maxworth Electronic Systems Pvt Ltd., is a maverick and looks at business very unlike a typical businessperson. His vision is to have a business which runs on ethics and benefits employees and society and does not look at abnormal profit making as a goal for his company. A technocrat who graduated in engineering from DCE and PGDM from IIM-B, he cut his teeth in the industry with Grindwell Norton (now part of Saint Gobain Group), Sales, Abrasives Division, 1983-88 and later Crompton Greaves Ltd., Telecom Division, Sales. Marketing & Product Management. In 1993 the entrepreneurial bug caught him, and he set up Maxworth along with his friend and colleague Sanjay Garg. A family man to the core, his wife and two daughters are the apple of his eyes. He has an abiding love of nature and devotes his spare time helping maintain the neighbourhood public parks.
